FC BORDEAUX'S STORY

Born in 1919, the Girondins de Bordeaux were a section of the omnisports club in Saint-Michel, in the heart of the Bordeaux district. It has known several years of glory, notably under the presidency of Claude Bez between 1978 and 1990. It also provided great players to the French team and won 6 times the French Championship, four French Cups and three League Cups. The Club played its first professional match on August 22, 1937 in Toulouse. After that, the Club managed to make a name for itself in French soccer, thanks to its victory in the French Cup in 1941 After a rather difficult period from 1972 to 1979, the Girondins dominated French soccer during the 1980s. The exceptional European epic is orchestrated by the magic trio Zidane, Dugarry and Lizarazu. That year, the Club played 20 matches on the European scene, including the incredible elimination of Milan A.C. in the quarterfinals. FC Bordeaux has known many legends: LIZARAZU, ZIDANE, PAULETA, TIGANA, DUGARRY.
